# Research Dossier Prompt v1

Generate an evidence-based research dossier from the provided brief.

Required sections:

1. Company summary (3-5 sentences)
2. Competitor landscape (5 competitors with differentiated positioning)
3. Technical constraints (at least 3)
4. Technical opportunities (at least 3)

Requirements:

- Tie findings to the specific company/industry context (no generic filler).
- Flag assumptions explicitly when evidence is incomplete.
- Present output in client-ready form suitable for downstream PRD/architecture planning.
